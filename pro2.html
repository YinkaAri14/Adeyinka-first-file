“The Landscape” Hotel

In Manhattan, New York, “The Landscape” uses organic shapes to create a continuous flow for biking pedestrians that leads from the High Line to the Little Island Park. These bike paths form the shape and structure of the building, creating a playful form that brings some of the Little Island’s gaiety to the Meatpacking District while also creating an engaging space to encourage bike use through the employment of an open street proposal.
