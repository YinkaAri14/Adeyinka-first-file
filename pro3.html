NOMA B.G.L Student Design Competition: S.T.I.T.C.H

The S.T.I.T.C.H. project—Stimulating, Thriving, Interactive, Transformative Community Healing—is a bold initiative designed to reconnect and revitalize Harlem Park. Through thoughtfully designed green spaces, affordable housing, and community hubs. The project stitches together the fractured north and south fabric of the neighborhood through key elements like a transit-oriented hub for connectivity, pedestiran pathways that encourage movement and interaction.
