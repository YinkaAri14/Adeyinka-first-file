My journey into architecture began in high school, where I dove into architectural drafting classes and earned certifications in AutoCAD, Inventor, and Photoshop. This early exposure sparked my passion for design and structures, leading me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Architecture with a minor in Construction Management at Louisiana State University. Now entering my 5th year, I’m grateful for the hands-on projects and valuable insights I’ve gained along the way. I’m a firm believer that dedication and hard work pay off in every project, and with each passing day, I continue to expand my knowledge and grow as a future architect.
